Support

How can we help?

Questions, feedback, or an issue with Pearlmeter? Email donnguyen@snug.com.

When reporting an issue, please include your iPhone model, iOS version, and what you expected to happen. Please do not email smile photos or other sensitive information.

Why does Pearlmeter need camera access?

The camera is used only when you start a smile-photo check-in. Your captured photos are processed and stored on your device and are not uploaded by the App.

How do I improve consistency?

Use the same room, lighting direction, camera distance, and smile position where practical. Card-free results are clearly labelled lower-confidence estimates. If you have a measured Pearlmeter reference card, you can use it as an optional consistency aid. Everyday lighting and camera differences can still affect visible appearance.

Do I need a reference card?

No. Choose “Continue without a card” to complete a guided check-in with no extra equipment. Use similar, even neutral lighting each time. The optional measured-card path can better account for camera colour differences.

Is the result a clinical tooth shade?

No. Pearlmeter compares your own check-ins and reports relative visible-appearance progress. It does not diagnose dental conditions or replace a dentist’s assessment.

How do I delete my data?

Open Settings in Pearlmeter and choose the delete-all-data option. You can also delete individual check-ins from the App. Because Pearlmeter has no account or server, support cannot retrieve or remotely delete data stored on your phone.

Camera access is unavailable

Open the iOS Settings app, find Pearlmeter, and allow Camera access. Then return to Pearlmeter and try the check-in again.
