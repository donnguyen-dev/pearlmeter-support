Privacy policy

Private by design.

Pearlmeter does not collect your data. Your smile photos, check-ins, and preferences remain on your device.

Last updated: 17 July 2026

The short version: no account, no login, no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no network connection. The developer cannot see your photos or check-ins.

Information stored on your device

When you use Pearlmeter, the App stores the information needed for your personal progress journal locally on your iPhone:

  • Smile photos you choose to capture and save
  • Check-in dates and locally calculated relative visible-appearance results
  • App preferences, such as onboarding completion

This information is not uploaded, transmitted, sold, or shared by Pearlmeter. The App contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

Camera access

Pearlmeter requests camera permission only when you choose to begin a capture. Camera access is used to photograph your smile for your journal. Captured photos are processed on your device and are not sent to us or to any third party.

Data collection

We collect nothing. Pearlmeter makes no network connections and the developer has no server or account through which to receive your information. This is consistent with the intended App Store “Data Not Collected” answer.

Your choices

You can delete individual check-ins or delete all Pearlmeter data from within the App. You can also remove camera permission in iOS Settings. Deleting the App removes its locally stored data from your device, subject to Apple’s device backup and restoration behaviour.

Health and medical information

Pearlmeter is a personal photo journal, not a medical device. It does not diagnose dental conditions, determine a clinical tooth shade, or provide medical or dental advice. Contact a qualified dental professional with questions about your oral health or treatment.

Children

Pearlmeter is not directed to children. We collect no information from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If Pearlmeter’s privacy practices change, this policy will be updated before the changed version is released. The date above will identify the latest revision.
